1. There are two different crawlers. One is to get article links of designated company from specific news providers. They are market watch.py and reuters_link.py.
2. market_crawler.py and reuters_crawler.py is to scrap news information and content by going to the individual URLs collected from the previous two scripts. 
3. The scraping process is done using Scrapinghub which allows users to deploy their spiders on the cloud platform and crawl 24/7.
